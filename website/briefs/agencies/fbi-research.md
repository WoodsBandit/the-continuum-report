# ANALYTICAL BRIEF: FEDERAL BUREAU OF INVESTIGATION

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

The FBI is the principal investigative arm of DOJ and a member of the Intelligence Community, established July 26, 1908. With ~$11.3B budget and 37,000+ positions, it leads domestic counterterrorism, counterintelligence, and major criminal investigations. Director Wray resigned January 2025; Kash Patel appointed.

---

## 1. OFFICIAL INFORMATION

**Full Name:** Federal Bureau of Investigation
**Founding:** July 26, 1908; named FBI July 1, 1935
**Former Director:** Christopher Wray (resigned January 10, 2025)
**Successor:** Kash Patel (January 2025)
**Headquarters:** Washington, DC

---

## 2. BUDGET

**FY2024:** $11.4 billion (direct budget authority)
**FY2025 Enacted:** ~$10.3-10.7 billion
**Historical Growth:** Tripled from $3.3B (FY2001) to $10.7B (FY2025)
**Staff:** 37,083 positions (13,623 special agents, 3,337 intel analysts, 20,123 professional staff)

---

## 4. KEY PROGRAMS

### Counterterrorism
- Leads Joint Terrorism Task Forces (200 JTTFs)
- 600+ state/local agencies, 50+ federal agencies integrated

### Counterintelligence
- Protects critical assets: defense, intelligence, economic, technology

### Cyber Division
- Lead federal agency for cyberattacks
- National Cyber Investigative Joint Task Force (30+ agencies)

### Criminal Investigations
- Violent crime, white-collar crime, civil rights violations

### Intelligence Community Role
- Full member of 17-agency IC
- Foreign Intelligence Collection Program (FICP)

---

## 5. DOCUMENTED CONTROVERSIES

### FISA Abuse
- Carter Page: Two of four warrants lacked probable cause
- 17 significant errors/omissions in applications
- ~4,000 Section 702 violations annually
- January 2025: Court ruled backdoor 702 searches require warrants

### Political Investigation Concerns
- Crossfire Hurricane (2016): Different standards for Clinton vs Trump campaigns
- Peter Strzok/Lisa Page texts showed political bias
- Durham Report (May 2023): FBI applied "raw, unanalyzed" intelligence

### Mar-a-Lago Search (August 2022)
- 13,000+ documents recovered, 337 classified
- Trump indicted on 40 counts; charges dropped December 2024

### Wray Resignation (January 2025)
- Resigned before Trump removal
- Trump called it "a great day for America"
- Only two FBI directors fired in history

---

## 6. RELATIONSHIPS

- DOJ: Principal investigative arm; reports to Attorney General
- IC: Full member; reports to DNI via ODNI
- CIA: Complementary foreign vs domestic focus
- NSA: Joint Section 702 oversight
- DHS: JTTF participation; HSI coordination
- State/Local: 200 JTTFs; 600+ participating agencies
