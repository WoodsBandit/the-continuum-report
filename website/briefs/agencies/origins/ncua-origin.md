# ORIGIN STORY: NATIONAL CREDIT UNION ADMINISTRATION

## FOUNDING DATE
June 26, 1934 (Federal Credit Union Act)
March 10, 1970 (NCUA as independent agency)

## FOUNDING LEGISLATION
Federal Credit Union Act (1934)
P.L. 91-206 (1970, created independent NCUA)

---

## KEY FOUNDERS

### EDWARD A. FILENE (Movement Father)

#### Background
- **Born:** September 3, 1860, Salem, Massachusetts
- **Died:** September 26, 1937
- **Career:** Built Filene's department stores; pioneered "Automatic Bargain Basement"; funded Credit Union National Extension Bureau

#### Religion
- **Jewish** (secular/non-practicing) - German-Jewish immigrant family; faced anti-Semitism

#### Politics
- **Party:** Democrat
- **Philosophy:** Progressive reformer; strong New Deal supporter; believed capitalism must operate more efficiently

### MORRIS SHEPPARD (Senate Sponsor)

#### Background
- **Born:** May 28, 1875, Morris County, Texas
- **Died:** April 9, 1941
- **Career:** Senator from Texas (1913-1941); descended from Robert Morris (Constitution signer)

#### Religion
- **Methodist** - Joined while at University of Texas

#### Politics
- **Party:** Democrat
- **Philosophy:** Progressive; author of 18th Amendment (Prohibition)

### WRIGHT PATMAN (House Sponsor)

#### Background
- **Born:** August 6, 1893, near Hughes Springs, Texas
- **Died:** March 7, 1976
- **Career:** WWI officer; Texas legislator; Congressman (24 terms); House Banking Committee Chairman

#### Religion
- **Primitive Baptist** (upbringing) - Mother "rigorous" in faith

#### Politics
- **Party:** Democrat
- **Philosophy:** Populist; opposed big banks, Federal Reserve, chain stores

### CLAUDE R. ORCHARD (1934-1953)

#### Background
- Executive at Armour & Company, Omaha
- Organized first Armour credit union
- Understood struggles of poorly-paid workers (17-18 cents/hour)

### HERMAN NICKERSON JR. (First NCUA Administrator, 1970-1976)

#### Background
- **Born:** 1913, Boston
- **Career:** USMC Lieutenant General; Distinguished Service Cross (Korea); commanded III Marine Amphibious Force (Vietnam)

## CONTEXT: CREDIT UNION MOVEMENT

### European Origins
- Started in Germany (1850s) - Schulze-Delitzsch and Raiffeisen cooperatives
- Spread to Canada (Alphonse Desjardins, 1900)
- First U.S. credit union: St. Mary's Bank, Manchester, NH (1908)

### Massachusetts Model (1909)
- Pierre Jay (later first Fed NY Chairman) helped draft first state law
- Filene financed expansion nationwide

### ROY BERGENGREN'S PHILOSOPHY
- "To teach people how to help themselves was more important by far in times of depression than at any other time"
- Rejected RFC offer of $100 million federal loans
- Feared converting "community enterprise into an agency of the government"

## MISSION

### Credit Union Oversight
- Charter federal credit unions
- Supervise and examine credit unions
- Administer National Credit Union Share Insurance Fund (NCUSIF)

### Philosophy
- Credit unions are member-owned cooperatives
- "Not for profit, not for charity, but for service"
- Democratic governance (one member, one vote)
