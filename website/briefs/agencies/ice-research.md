# ANALYTICAL BRIEF: IMMIGRATION AND CUSTOMS ENFORCEMENT

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

ICE is DHS's principal investigative and immigration enforcement arm, established March 1, 2003. With unprecedented $28.7B+ FY2025 funding (potentially $74.9B total), it operates through ERO (removals) and HSI (investigations). Faces major 2025 enforcement expansion and detention controversies.

---

## 1. OFFICIAL INFORMATION

**Full Name:** U.S. Immigration and Customs Enforcement
**Founding:** March 1, 2003 (Homeland Security Act)
**Acting Director:** Todd Lyons (2025)
**Parent Agency:** Department of Homeland Security
**Staff:** 20,000+ personnel; 400+ offices

---

## 2. BUDGET

**FY2024:** ~$9.4-9.56 billion
**FY2025 Initial:** $9.31 billion
**FY2025 Total:** $28.7 billion (with supplementals)
**Potential Total:** $74.9 billion (multi-year authorities)
**Detention Allocation:** ~$45 billion of DHS budget

---

## 3. ORGANIZATIONAL STRUCTURE

**Enforcement and Removal Operations (ERO):**
- Immigration enforcement lifecycle
- ~250,000+ removals annually to 150+ countries
- 25 field offices
- Custody Management, Removal Operations

**Homeland Security Investigations (HSI):**
- Principal DHS investigative component
- ~8,700 employees
- 237+ domestic offices, 93+ international
- Transnational crime, terrorism, exploitation

**Supporting Divisions:**
- Office of Principal Legal Advisor (OPLA)
- Office of Professional Responsibility
- Management & Program Administration

---

## 4. KEY PROGRAMS

### Immigration Enforcement
- Detention: 68,440 in custody (December 2025)
- 287(g) Program: 1,000+ participating agencies (from 0 in January 2025)
- Alternatives to Detention (ATD)
- Worksite Raids: 40+ actions, 1,100+ arrests (first 7 months 2025)

### HSI Investigations
- Transnational criminal organizations
- Human trafficking (Center for Countering Human Trafficking)
- Trade-based money laundering
- Cybercrime
- Joint Task Force Alpha (JTFA)

---

## 5. DOCUMENTED CONTROVERSIES

### Detention Conditions
- Courts cite inadequate sleeping space, bedding, meals
- Broadview facility: U.S. citizen detained in "filthy cell"
- Dilley: Worms/mold in food; child self-harm
- "Alligator Alcatraz" (Florida): ACLU lawsuit

### 287(g) Program Expansion
- ACLU: "System of racial profiling"
- Creates community distrust
- Discourages crime reporting
- Joe Arpaio lawsuit: Unconstitutional Latino roundups

### Workplace Raids
- Southeastern Provision: Latino workers targeted with slurs
- Postville, Iowa: All Latino-presumed handcuffed
- Fourth Amendment violation risks
- Minimal employer accountability

### Sanctuary City Conflicts (2025)
- NYC: 6,025 arrest requests since January 2025 (400%+ increase)
- Chicago: Mayor established "ICE Free Zones"
- Boston: DOJ lawsuit for sanctuary ordinances
- Trump EOs threaten funding to sanctuary jurisdictions

### HSI-ERO Tension
- 2018: 19 HSI SACs requested formal separation from ICE
- Criminal investigations vs civil enforcement conflict

---

## 6. RELATIONSHIPS

- DHS: Parent agency; second-largest investigative component
- CBP: Sister agency; border vs interior enforcement
- DOJ (EOIR): Immigration court proceedings
- FBI: Joint Terrorism Task Forces
- State/Local: 287(g), Secure Communities, database integration
