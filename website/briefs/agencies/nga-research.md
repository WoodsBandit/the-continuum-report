# ANALYTICAL BRIEF: NATIONAL GEOSPATIAL-INTELLIGENCE AGENCY

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

The National Geospatial-Intelligence Agency (NGA) is a combat support agency within DoD and principal IC member, serving as world leader in geospatial intelligence (GEOINT). NGA collects, analyzes, and distributes intelligence derived from imagery and geospatial information with approximately 14,500 personnel across 100+ U.S. locations.

---

## 1. OFFICIAL INFORMATION

**Full Name:** National Geospatial-Intelligence Agency (NGA)

**Predecessor:** National Imagery and Mapping Agency (NIMA), 1996

**Renamed:** November 24, 2003

**Current Director:** Army Lieutenant General Michelle Bredenkamp

**Headquarters:** Fort Belvoir North Area, Springfield, Virginia (2.3 million sq ft)
