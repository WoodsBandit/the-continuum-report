## The Documented Record

### Robert Maxwell's Death and Israeli State Funeral

Robert Maxwell died November 5, 1991, after falling from his yacht, the *Lady Ghislaine*, off the coast of the Canary Islands. He was buried in Israel on the Mount of Olives in Jerusalem on November 10, 1991.

The funeral was attended by Israeli government officials, including then-Prime Minister Yitzhak Shamir and President Chaim Herzog. Multiple published accounts have documented the presence of intelligence officials at the ceremony.

### Congressional Investigation Reference

House Judiciary Committee "The INSLAW Affair" (September 1992):

The committee report examined testimony and affidavits regarding alleged PROMIS software distribution. Robert Maxwell's name appeared in connection with allegations that modified versions of the PROMIS software were distributed through his publishing companies to foreign intelligence services.

### Ari Ben-Menashe Testimony

Ari Ben-Menashe, who claimed to be a former Israeli military intelligence officer, provided testimony examined by the House Judiciary Committee. Ben-Menashe made allegations regarding Robert Maxwell's relationship with Israeli intelligence services.
