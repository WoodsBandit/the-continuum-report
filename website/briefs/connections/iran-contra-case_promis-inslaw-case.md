## Alternative Interpretations

1. **Separate matters:** Iran-Contra and INSLAW were investigated by different congressional committees and involved different alleged misconduct. Temporal overlap does not establish operational connection.

2. **Different agencies:** Iran-Contra centered on the NSC staff and CIA; INSLAW centered on DOJ contract administration. These are distinct institutional actors.

3. **Different outcomes:** Iran-Contra resulted in criminal prosecutions (later pardoned); INSLAW resulted in no criminal prosecutions despite judicial findings of fraud.

4. **Correlation vs. causation:** Both occurring during the same administration may reflect the era's political climate rather than coordinated activities.

5. **Evidence limitations:** Congressional investigations did not establish direct operational links between Iran-Contra participants and INSLAW misconduct.
