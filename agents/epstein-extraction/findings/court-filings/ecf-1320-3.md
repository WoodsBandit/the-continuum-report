## Summary
This is a transcript excerpt from Ghislaine Maxwell's videotaped deposition on April 22, 2016, focusing on her interactions with Johanna Sjoberg. Maxwell testified that Sjoberg was hired to answer telephones and acknowledged that Sjoberg gave her massages, stating there was no sex involved. However, when asked about sexual contact with Sjoberg, Maxwell's attorney instructed her not to answer questions about "consensual sex," and Maxwell complied, though she did deny having nonconsensual sex with anyone including Annie Farmer.
