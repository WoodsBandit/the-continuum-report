## Legal Compliance Framework

### Milkovich Standard Implementation

The enhanced template implements the *Milkovich v. Lorain Journal Co.* framework for protected opinion:

1. **Disclosed Facts:** All factual claims attributed to specific court documents with ECF citations
2. **Clear Opinion Labels:** "Editorial Analysis" sections explicitly marked throughout
3. **Opinion Qualifiers:** "In our assessment," "Based on our review," "We observe that"
4. **Alternative Interpretations:** Multiple competing frameworks provided for each connection
5. **Fair Report Privilege:** Accurate reporting of official proceedings and court filings

### Fifth Amendment Context

The template integrates Fifth Amendment considerations:

- Presumption of innocence clearly stated
- Lack of criminal charges noted
- Allegations distinguished from proven facts
- Categorical denials documented where applicable

### Source Attribution

All factual claims properly attributed:

- Direct quotes from court documents
- ECF citation numbers with filing dates
- Clickable PDF viewer links for verification
- Document index for comprehensive source list
