# ANALYTICAL BRIEF: UNITED STATES SECRET SERVICE

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

The Secret Service is a federal law enforcement agency within DHS (since 2003), established July 5, 1865. With ~$3.2B budget and 8,100+ employees, it handles presidential protection and financial crimes investigation. Faces critical staffing shortages and institutional failures exposed by July 2024 Trump assassination attempt.

---

## 1. OFFICIAL INFORMATION

**Full Name:** United States Secret Service (USSS)
**Founding:** July 5, 1865 (originally Secret Service Division of Treasury)
**Current Director:** Sean M. Curran (28th Director, sworn in January 22, 2025)
**Parent Agency:** Department of Homeland Security (transferred March 1, 2003, from Treasury)
**Staff:** 8,100+ employees

---

## 2. BUDGET

**FY2025:** ~$3.2 billion (8,300 authorized positions)
**FY2024:** ~$3.3 billion
**Historical Growth:** Doubled from $1.8B (FY2014) to $3.2B+ (FY2025)
**FY2025 Supplemental:** $231 million for 2024 campaign protective operations

## 4. KEY PROGRAMS

### Presidential Protection
- Protects President, VP, families, former Presidents, visiting heads of state
- Secures White House Complex and VP residence
- Coordinates National Special Security Events (NSSEs)

### Financial Crimes Investigations
- Counterfeiting, bank fraud, illicit financing
- Identity theft, intellectual property crimes
- Focus on large, high-dollar impact organized crime

### Cyber Investigations
- Cyber Investigative Section (CIS): Central cyber-enabled financial crime coordination
- Cyber Fraud Task Forces (CFTFs): 42 domestic + 2 international locations
- Plans to expand to 160 offices globally

## 5. DOCUMENTED CONTROVERSIES

### July 13, 2024 Trump Assassination Attempt (Butler, PA)
- Shooter climbed onto roof undetected despite intelligence warnings
- Gunman spotted ~90 minutes prior; not encountered
- Suspicious individual with rangefinder reported 25+ minutes before; not relayed to protective detail
- Building where shooter positioned was not secured
- Failures: communication breakdowns, command/control deficiencies, advance planning gaps
- Independent review: "Deep flaws" and "troubling lack of critical thinking"
- Accountability: 6 personnel suspended (10-42 days); no terminations
- Former Director Kimberly Cheatle resigned
- False congressional testimony: Cheatle denied resource requests were denied

### 2024 Sexual Misconduct Incidents
- September 2024: Agent in Harris detail groped staffer while intoxicated
- Joint Base Andrews: Female agent had mental breakdown, attacked supervisor
- Congressional investigation opened on "inadequate training"

### 2012 Colombia Prostitution Scandal (Historical)
- 11 personnel involved in prostitution solicitation during Obama security detail
- 8 of 12 employees lost jobs
- Similar conduct documented in El Salvador, Panama, China, Romania
