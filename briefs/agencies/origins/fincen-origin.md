## CONTEXT: MONEY LAUNDERING CRISIS

### Bank Secrecy Act (1970)
- Required financial institutions to report cash transactions
- Created reporting framework FinCEN would later use

### Purpose of FinCEN
- Provide multi-source intelligence network
- Support detection, investigation, prosecution of money laundering
- Analyze financial data for patterns of illegal activity

### Bureau Status (2002)
- Treasury Order 180-01 (September 26, 2002)
- Elevated to bureau status after PATRIOT Act

---

## MISSION

### Financial Intelligence
- Collects and analyzes financial transaction data
- Supports law enforcement investigations
- Administers Bank Secrecy Act compliance

### Anti-Money Laundering
- Suspicious Activity Reports (SARs)
- Currency Transaction Reports (CTRs)
- Information sharing with law enforcement

### Modern Role
- Combats terrorist financing
- Enforces sanctions
- Cryptocurrency regulation
