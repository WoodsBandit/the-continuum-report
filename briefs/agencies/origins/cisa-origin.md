## KEY FOUNDERS

### MICHAEL McCAUL (Legislative Sponsor)

#### Background
- **Born:** January 14, 1962, Dallas, Texas
- **Career:** Chief of Counterterrorism, U.S. Attorney's Office; Deputy Attorney General of Texas; Chairman, House Homeland Security Committee

#### Religion
- **Roman Catholic** (devout; attended Jesuit preparatory school)

#### Politics
- **Party:** Republican
- **Philosophy:** Strong national defense; bipartisan on national security; member of Republican Study Committee

### KIRSTJEN NIELSEN (DHS Secretary)

#### Background
- **Born:** May 14, 1972, Colorado Springs, Colorado
- **Career:** Corporate attorney; Bush administration; DHS Secretary (2017-2019)

#### Religion
- Not documented

#### Politics
- Served in Republican administrations
- Statement: "National security is bipartisan. Homeland security is bipartisan. I don't get into the politics of it."

## CONTEXT: CYBER THREATS

### Predecessor
- National Protection and Programs Directorate (NPPD), formed 2007
- Needed elevated status and clearer mission

### Threats Addressed
- State-sponsored cyberattacks
- Critical infrastructure vulnerabilities
- Election security
- Ransomware and cybercrime

### Bipartisan Support
- House: Passed unanimously
- Senate: Passed by unanimous consent

## MISSION

### Cybersecurity
- Defend federal networks

- Share threat intelligence

### Infrastructure Security
- Protect critical infrastructure (16 sectors)

- Emergency communications
