## Summary
This is a motion to compel filed by Virginia Giuffre's attorneys on May 5, 2016, seeking to force Ghislaine Maxwell to answer deposition questions about sexual activities with Jeffrey Epstein that Maxwell refused to answer during her April 22, 2016 deposition. The plaintiff argues that Maxwell's participation in and knowledge of Epstein's pattern of using massages as cover for sexual abuse is central to proving the defamation case and corroborating Giuffre's allegations.
