# ANALYTICAL BRIEF: CONSUMER FINANCIAL PROTECTION BUREAU

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

The CFPB is an independent consumer financial protection agency created by Dodd-Frank (2010). Uniquely funded through Federal Reserve transfers (not appropriations), it faces existential threat from 2025 administration efforts to dismantle the agency.

---

## 1. OFFICIAL INFORMATION

**Full Name:** Consumer Financial Protection Bureau
**Founding:** July 2010 (Dodd-Frank Act); launched July 2011
**Acting Director:** Russell Vought (February 2025)
**Previous Director:** Rohit Chopra (fired February 1, 2025)
**Headquarters:** 1700 G Street NW, Washington, DC

---

## 2. BUDGET/FUNDING

**Funding:** Federal Reserve transfers (not appropriations)
**Cap:** 12% of Fed operating expenses
**Constitutional Challenge:** SCOTUS ruled 7-2 constitutional (May 2024)
**2025 Status:** P.L. 119-21 reduced draw by ~46%

---

## 3. ORGANIZATIONAL STRUCTURE

**Single Director** (appointed by President, Senate confirmed)

**Divisions:**
1. Supervision Division
2. Enforcement Division
3. Consumer Response and Education
4. Research, Monitoring & Regulations
5. Operations Division
6. Legal Division
7. External Affairs

**Specialized Offices:** Fair Lending, Civil Rights, OMWI, Ombudsman

---

## 4. KEY PROGRAMS

- Consumer Complaint Database (launched 2012)
- Over $21 billion returned to consumers
- Mortgage regulation (TILA, RESPA, ECOA)
- Credit card oversight
- Payday lending rules
- Fair lending enforcement

---

## 5. DOCUMENTED CONTROVERSIES

### Constitutional Challenge (2022-2024)
- Fifth Circuit: Funding unconstitutional
- SCOTUS (May 2024): Reversed 7-2, constitutional

### Trump First Term Gutting (2017-2021)
- Mulvaney: Called CFPB "a joke"
- Stripped enforcement authority
- Fired advisory board

### Trump Second Term Dismantling (2025)
- February 1-7: Rapid director changes
- February 10: All staff ordered to cease work
- April: ~1,400 layoff notices (95% workforce)
- Planned: Delete databases, return funding to Fed
- Court intervention: Preliminary injunction issued

### Industry Opposition
- Banking/payday lending industry support elimination
- Project 2025 supports defunding
- Elon Musk: "CFPB RIP"

### Legal Battles (2025)
- 21 states + DC sued to prevent defunding
- December 2025: Federal court blocked defunding

---

## 6. RELATIONSHIPS

- Federal Reserve: Funding source, within Fed system
- OCC, FDIC, NCUA: Supervision coordination MOU
- FTC: Concurrent jurisdiction, cooperation agreement
- Fair Lending Task Force: HUD, FTC, FDIC, Fed, OCC, DOJ, FHFA
