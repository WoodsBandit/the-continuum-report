# ORIGIN STORY: NATIONAL OCEANIC AND ATMOSPHERIC ADMINISTRATION

## FOUNDING DATE
October 3, 1970

## FOUNDING LEGISLATION
Reorganization Plan No. 4 of 1970

---

## KEY FOUNDER

### RICHARD M. NIXON (President)

#### Background
- **Born:** January 9, 1913, Yorba Linda, California
- **Died:** April 22, 1994
- **Career:** Navy officer; Congressman; Senator; Vice President; President

#### Religion
- **Quaker** - raised in devout Quaker household

#### Politics
- **Party:** Republican
- **Philosophy:** Pragmatic; surprisingly progressive on environmental issues

### Nixon's Environmental Year (1970)
- January 1, 1970: Signed National Environmental Policy Act (NEPA)
- April 22, 1970: First Earth Day
- July 1970: Proposed NOAA
- October 3, 1970: NOAA established
- December 1970: EPA established

---

## CONTEXT: ENVIRONMENTAL AWAKENING

### First Earth Day (April 1970)
- 20 million Americans participated
- Bipartisan environmental consciousness
- Public demanded government action
- Nixon responded with new agencies

### Ocean and Atmosphere Recognition
- Oceans and atmosphere interconnected
- Climate, weather, fisheries needed unified approach
- Scattered programs across multiple agencies inefficient
- Scientific understanding required coordination

---

## CREATION OF NOAA

### Reorganization Plan No. 4
- July 1970: Nixon submitted plan to Congress
- Consolidated multiple agencies and programs
- October 3, 1970: NOAA established
- Placed within Department of Commerce

### Agencies Consolidated Into NOAA
- **U.S. Weather Bureau** (Commerce)
- **Coast and Geodetic Survey** (nation's oldest scientific agency)
- **Bureau of Commercial Fisheries** (Interior)
- **Environmental Data Service**
- **National Oceanographic Data Center**
- Sea Grant Program
- Marine minerals research
- Other related programs

## PREDECESSOR AGENCIES

### Coast and Geodetic Survey
- Founded 1807 by Thomas Jefferson
- Oldest U.S. scientific agency
- Surveyed coastline; made nautical charts

### Weather Bureau
- Established 1870 under Army Signal Corps
- Moved to Agriculture 1890
- Moved to Commerce 1940
- National weather forecasting

### Bureau of Commercial Fisheries
- Managed fisheries research
- From Department of Interior
- Became NOAA Fisheries (NMFS)

## SIGNIFICANCE

### Climate Science
- Primary U.S. climate research agency
- Monitors greenhouse gases
- Tracks climate trends
- Provides climate data globally

### Weather Prediction
- National Weather Service within NOAA
- Hurricane forecasting
- Tornado warnings
- Daily forecasts for all Americans

### Ocean Stewardship
- Manages fisheries
- Protects marine mammals
- Charts nautical waters
- Monitors ocean conditions
