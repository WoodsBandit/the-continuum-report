## CATALYST: STUDEBAKER CRISIS

### What Happened
- December 1963: Studebaker plant closed in South Bend, Indiana
- 8,500+ auto workers affected
- Company had borrowed from pension fund to stay afloat
- Plan severely underfunded when terminated

### Impact on Workers
- Employees under 60 (including 40-year veterans) received ~15% of promised pension
- Employees under 40 received nothing
- Workers lost lifetime savings with no recourse

### Response
- UAW President Walter Reuther proposed "pension reinsurance"
- Javits introduced first bill in 1966
- 8 years later, ERISA/PBGC enacted

## LEGISLATIVE ACHIEVEMENT

### Bipartisan Support
- Senate vote: 85-0 (unanimous)
- House vote: Near-unanimous (only 2 against)

### ERISA Provisions
- Established pension funding standards
- Created PBGC as government corporation
- Set vesting requirements
- Required fiduciary responsibility

## JAVITS ON CONSTITUTION

### Views
- Committed to making constitutional guarantees of equal protection "a reality to every individual"
- Co-authored War Powers Resolution (1973) to limit presidential war-making
- Believed both parties should tolerate diverse opinions

---

## MISSION

### Pension Insurance
- Protects pension benefits for ~31 million workers
- Insures defined benefit pension plans
- Pays benefits when plans fail
- Self-financed through premiums (no taxpayer funding)
