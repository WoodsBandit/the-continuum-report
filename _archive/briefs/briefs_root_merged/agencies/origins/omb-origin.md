# ORIGIN STORY: OFFICE OF MANAGEMENT AND BUDGET

## FOUNDING DATE
June 10, 1921 (Bureau of the Budget)
1970 (Renamed OMB under Nixon)

## FOUNDING LEGISLATION
Budget and Accounting Act of 1921 (P.L. 67-13)

---

## KEY FOUNDERS

### JAMES WILLIAM GOOD (Legislative Sponsor)

#### Background
- **Born:** September 24, 1866, near Cedar Rapids, Iowa
- **Died:** November 18, 1929
- **Career:** Lawyer; Chairman, House Appropriations Committee; later Secretary of War under Hoover

#### Religion
- Not documented

#### Politics
- **Party:** Republican
- **Philosophy:** Conservative; fiscal discipline

### CHARLES GATES DAWES (First Director)

#### Background
- **Born:** August 27, 1865, Marietta, Ohio
- **Died:** April 23, 1951
- **Career:** Lawyer; banker; WWI general; first Budget Director; Nobel Peace Prize (1925); Vice President (1925-1929)
- **Family:** Descendant of William Dawes (rode with Paul Revere)

#### Religion
- **Presbyterian**

#### Politics
- **Party:** Republican
- **Philosophy:** Small government; efficiency; opposed FDR's spending; campaigned against KKK

### Constitutional Views
- Called Senate filibuster rules "inimical to the principles of our constitutional government"

## CONTEXT: PROGRESSIVE ERA REFORM

### Before 1921
- No unified federal budget
- Each agency submitted requests directly to Congress
- No presidential coordination
- Called "bookkeeping of a lunatic" by Taft

### Taft Commission (1911-1913)
- First serious study of federal budget reform
- Recommended executive budget
- Congress initially rejected

### 1921 Act
- Warren Harding called it "the greatest reform in governmental practices since the beginning of the republic"
- Created Bureau of the Budget in Treasury Department
- Moved to Executive Office of President (1939)

---

## TRANSFORMATION TO OMB (1970)

### Nixon's Reorganization
- Added "Management" function
- Gave OMB greater power over agencies
- Became one of most powerful EOP offices

---

## MISSION

### Budget Formulation
- Prepares President's budget proposal
- Reviews agency budget requests
- Enforces spending limits

### Management Oversight
- Reviews regulations
- Coordinates administration policy
- Clearance of legislative proposals
