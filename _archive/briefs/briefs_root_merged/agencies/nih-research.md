# ANALYTICAL BRIEF: NATIONAL INSTITUTES OF HEALTH

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

NIH is the nation's medical research agency, established 1887. With ~$48.5B budget and 27 institutes/centers, it funds 82% of budget through extramural grants and operates the world's largest biomedical research institution. Faces gain-of-function research controversy, Wuhan/EcoHealth funding revelations, and 40% proposed FY2026 budget cuts.

---

## 1. OFFICIAL INFORMATION

**Full Name:** National Institutes of Health (NIH)
**Founding:** 1887 (Hygienic Laboratory); renamed 1930 (Ransdell Act)
**Current Director:** Dr. Jay Bhattacharya, M.D., Ph.D. (18th Director, April 2025)
**Parent Agency:** Department of Health and Human Services (HHS)
**Headquarters:** Bethesda, Maryland (primary); Research Triangle Park, NC

---

## 2. BUDGET

**FY2024:** $48.6 billion
**FY2025:** $48.495 billion (includes $1.5B ARPA-H)
**FY2026 Proposed:** $27.915 billion (40.6% cut)
**Budget Composition:**
- 82-83% extramural research grants
- ~10% intramural research
- Remaining: clinical center, administration

## 4. KEY PROGRAMS

### Extramural Research (~$39.6B, 82-83%)
- ~50,000 competitive grants annually
- Distributed to universities, research institutions
- Administered by Office of Extramural Research

### Intramural Research Program (~$3-4B, ~10%)
- Largest biomedical research institution on earth
- 1,200 Principal Investigators
- 4,000+ Postdoctoral Fellows
- 23 of 27 institutes have intramural components

### NIH Clinical Center
- World's largest clinical research hospital
- "Bench to bedside" translation
- Rare disease treatment

## 5. DOCUMENTED CONTROVERSIES

### Gain-of-Function Research & Wuhan Institute
- 2014 Grant: $3.4M to EcoHealth; $598,500 subaward to Wuhan Institute of Virology
- May 2024: NIH admitted U.S. taxpayers funded gain-of-function research at WIV
- Contradicted prior Fauci testimony (2021)
- WIV withheld laboratory notebooks
- August 2022: NIH terminated WIV subaward
- HHS began EcoHealth debarment proceedings

### Fauci/NIAID Controversy
- 2021: Fauci denied NIH funded gain-of-function in Wuhan
- 2024 testimony contradicted this admission
- Dr. David Morens allegedly obstructed investigations
- $710 million in pharmaceutical royalties to NIH scientists during pandemic
