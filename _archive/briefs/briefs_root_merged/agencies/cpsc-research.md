# ANALYTICAL BRIEF: CONSUMER PRODUCT SAFETY COMMISSION

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

The CPSC is an independent agency regulating consumer product safety, established October 27, 1972. With ~$151M budget and jurisdiction over 15,000+ product types, it oversees recalls, safety standards, and import surveillance. Faces underfunding and proposed absorption into HHS.

---

## 1. OFFICIAL INFORMATION

**Full Name:** U.S. Consumer Product Safety Commission
**Founding:** October 27, 1972 (Consumer Product Safety Act)
**Current Chair:** Peter A. Feldman (Acting, January 2025)
**Headquarters:** Bethesda, Maryland
**Regional Offices:** Chicago, New York, San Francisco

## 4. KEY PROGRAMS

### Product Recalls
- Nearly all recalls voluntary
- Fast Track Program: Streamlined process for cooperative firms
- 275 of 465 recalls were Fast Track (2009)

### Safety Standards
- Mandatory and voluntary standards development
- Jurisdiction over 15,000+ consumer product types
- Section 15(b) reporting requirements

### NEISS (National Electronic Injury Surveillance System)
- ~100 hospital probability sample
- Collects product-related injury data
- Generates national injury estimates

### Import Surveillance
- Office of Import Surveillance (EXIS)
- Collocated with CBP at major ports
- FY2021: ~$996B in CPSC-regulated imports

## 5. DOCUMENTED CONTROVERSIES

### Gas Stove Controversy (2023)
- January 2023: Commissioner Trumka called gas stoves "hidden hazard"
- Chair clarified: Not looking to ban
- House passed Gas Stove Protection Act (symbolic)
- Health claims: 40M+ households; linked to respiratory disease
- Political backlash: Senator Manchin called it "recipe for disaster"

### Amazon Marketplace Liability (2024)
- July 2024: CPSC found Amazon "distributor" of 400,000 hazardous products
- Products: Flammability violations, CO detector failures, lead toys
- Many from foreign sellers; CPSC limited enforcement options

### Peloton Treadmill (2021)
- Tread+ entrapment hazard; 150+ reports, one child death
- Peloton delayed reporting, initially resisted recall
- 2023: $19.065 million civil penalty

### Lead in Children's Products
- Recurring issue with imports (86% from China)
- Average 25 months on market before recall
- CPSIA 2008 lowered lead limits significantly

### Underfunding Crisis
- Critical vacancies: Compliance Director, CFO, Auditor
- Proposed FY2026: 14% staff cut (534→459 FTE)
- Proposed HHS absorption would end independent status
