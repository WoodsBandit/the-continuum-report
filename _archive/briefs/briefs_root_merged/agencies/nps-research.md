# ANALYTICAL BRIEF: NATIONAL PARK SERVICE

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

The NPS manages 433 park units across 85 million acres, established August 25, 1916. With ~$3.3B budget and 20,000+ employees, it preserves natural and cultural resources while providing recreation. Faces $23 billion maintenance backlog, record visitation, staffing crisis, and controversial 2025 policy changes.

---

## 1. OFFICIAL INFORMATION

**Full Name:** National Park Service (NPS)
**Founding:** August 25, 1916 (National Park Service Organic Act)
**Current Director:** Jessica Bowron (Acting, 2025)
**Parent Agency:** Department of the Interior
**Headquarters:** Washington, D.C.
**Staff:** 20,000+ employees

---

## 2. BUDGET

**FY2025:** $3.325 billion (7% below request)
**FY2024:** $3.325 billion (-4% from FY2023)
**Operations (ONPS):** $2.894 billion (87% of budget)
**Historic Preservation Fund:** $188.7 million
**Construction:** $172.3 million
**Mandatory Funding:** ~$1.240 billion (fees, donations, concessions)

## 4. KEY PROGRAMS

### Park Management
- 433 park units, 19 naming designations
- 85+ million acres managed
- Resource stewardship and protection

### Visitor Services
- 331.9 million recreational visits (2024 record)
- Timed-entry systems at 9 parks
- Interpretive programming, ranger services

### Historic Preservation
- Historic Preservation Fund grants
- Save America's Treasures program
- American Battlefield Protection Program

### Climate Response
- Climate Change Response Program
- 57% of parks at high risk from fire, drought, pests
- $700 million IRA allocation for climate resilience

---

## 5. DOCUMENTED CONTROVERSIES

### $23 Billion Maintenance Backlog
- $22.986 billion deferred maintenance (FY2024)
- 102% growth over past decade
- 71,000+ assets requiring maintenance
- Great American Outdoors Act provides only $1.33B annually

### Overcrowding
- 331.9 million visits in 2024 (all-time high)
- Traffic congestion, worn trails, environmental degradation
- Timed-entry causing 15-25% lodging revenue drops in gateway counties

### Staffing Crisis (2025)
- 24% of permanent staff lost since Trump administration began
- 1,000 probationary workers cut
- Single rangers staffing entrance gates all season
- Interpretive programming reduced or halted

### 2025 Policy Changes
- MLK Jr. Day and Juneteenth removed from fee-free calendar
- Trump's birthday (June 14) added as fee-free day
- International visitor surcharges: $100 extra at 11 major parks
- Annual pass: $250 for non-residents vs. $80 for residents
- LGBTQ content removed from Stonewall National Monument page
- Climate change educational signs removed from Acadia
- America the Beautiful pass redesigned with Trump portrait

## 6. RELATIONSHIPS

- Interior: Parent agency; Director reports to Secretary

- Fish and Wildlife Service: Wilderness and ESA coordination
- National Interagency Fire Center: Fire response coordination since 1970s
