In our assessment, the fact that only monitors, keyboards, and cables remained when police arrived suggests deliberate removal of the devices that would contain data.

**Regarding Banasiak's role:** Banasiak appears in the documentary record solely as a witness who observed events. His testimony was taken under oath and has been cited in multiple court filings. He has never been accused of wrongdoing.
