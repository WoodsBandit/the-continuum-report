# ORIGIN STORY: U.S. FISH AND WILDLIFE SERVICE

## FOUNDING DATE
June 30, 1940 (Reorganization Plan III)

## FOUNDING LEGISLATION
Reorganization Plan No. III of 1940

---

## KEY FOUNDERS

### SPENCER FULLERTON BAIRD (Fish Commission Founder, 1871)

#### Background
- **Born:** February 3, 1823, Reading, Pennsylvania
- **Died:** August 19, 1887
- **Career:** Professor at Dickinson College; Smithsonian Secretary; First U.S. Fish Commissioner

#### Religion
- Not documented

#### Politics
- Not documented (family had political connections)

### CLINTON HART MERRIAM (Biological Survey Builder)

#### Background
- **Born:** December 5, 1855, New York City
- **Died:** March 19, 1942
- **Career:** Physician turned naturalist; headed Bureau of Biological Survey predecessor (1885-1910); "father of mammalogy"; co-founded National Geographic Society

#### Religion
- Not documented

#### Politics
- Father was Republican Congressman from New York

### JAY "DING" DARLING (Modern Conservation Architect)

#### Background
- **Born:** October 21, 1876, Norwood, Michigan
- **Died:** February 12, 1962
- **Career:** Two-time Pulitzer Prize cartoonist; Director of Bureau of Biological Survey (1934-1935); created Federal Duck Stamp program; founded National Wildlife Federation

#### Religion
- **Congregationalist background** - Son of Congregational minister

#### Politics
- **Party:** Republican
- **Philosophy:** Conservative; "Hoover Republican"; distrusted FDR and New Deal

## PREDECESSOR AGENCIES

### Bureau of Fisheries (1871)
- Originally U.S. Fish Commission
- Under Department of Commerce
- Founded by Spencer Baird

### Bureau of Biological Survey (1885)
- Originally Section of Economic Ornithology
- Under Department of Agriculture
- Built by Clinton Hart Merriam

### FDR's Merger (1940)
- Transferred both to Interior Department (1939)
- Merged into single Fish and Wildlife Service (1940)
- FDR created 140 wildlife refuges during presidency

---

## DING DARLING'S LEGACY

### Duck Stamp Program
- Darling designed first Federal Duck Stamp (1934)
- Hunters must purchase annually
- Funds wildlife refuge acquisition
- Over $1 billion raised to date

### National Wildlife Federation
- Founded by Darling in 1936
- Now largest conservation organization in America

### Conservative Conservationist
- Darling lampooned FDR's New Deal in cartoons
- Yet accepted appointment from FDR to lead conservation
- Showed conservation transcends partisan divisions

---

## MISSION

### Wildlife Management
- Manages 568+ National Wildlife Refuges
- Enforces wildlife laws (Endangered Species Act, Migratory Bird Treaty Act)
- Conserves fish and wildlife habitats

### Endangered Species
- Lists and protects endangered/threatened species
- Develops recovery plans
- Partners with states and tribes
